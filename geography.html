Coast

The Coast is a narrow desert strip along the Pacific Ocean, home to cities like Lima. Despite its arid climate, it supports agriculture through river valleys.

Amazon Rainforest

The Amazon Rainforest covers nearly 60% of Peru, making it one of the most biodiverse places on Earth. It is home to unique wildlife and indigenous communities.
