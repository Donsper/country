Inti Raymi

Catholicism is the dominant religion, but Indigenous beliefs remain strong, leading to unique celebrations like Inti Raymi, an ancient Incan festival honoring the sun god.

Ceviche

Peruvian food is world-renowned, with dishes like ceviche (marinated seafood) and lomo saltado (stir-fried beef with vegetables) reflecting a mix of Spanish and Indigenous flavors.
