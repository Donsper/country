Welcome to Peru

Peru is a country in South America, known for its stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and ancient civilizations.

It's home to the famous Machu Picchu, a breathtaking Incan citadel, and the vibrant capital city of Lima. The country's geography ranges from the Pacific coastline to the towering Andes Mountains and the lush Amazon Rainforest.
